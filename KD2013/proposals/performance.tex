\abstract{Overview of tools and best practices and methods for
  testing, analyzing test data, reporting, and troubleshooting
  performance in Kuali Applications. In Kuali Days 2012, there was a
  session that focused on performance testing KFS for UConn. This
  session expands and improves on that session by providing actual
  results, concrete examples, and proven methods. Further, the topic
  of automation and maintenance will be addressed. }

\section{Objectives}
\begin{itemize}
\item Overview of rSmart performance testing tools.
\item Analyze strategies to performance testing.
\item Explanation of caveats we encountered.
\item Analyze reports from scenario executions.
\item Review the improvements made and the results from further testing.
\end{itemize}
